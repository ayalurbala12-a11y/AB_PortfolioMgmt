Quick Start

  1. Log a trade — go to Open Trades → fill in the "Log New Trade" form (date, ticker, strategy, action, qty, expiry, strike, entry premium, fees, brokerage). Hit Add Trade.
  2. Close a trade — in the Open Trades table, click any trade ID to open its modal. Switch to the "Close Trade" view, enter close date, action (STC/BTC/EXP/ASS), close premium, and close fee.
  3. Bulk import — go to Import CSV → drop a CSV with the listed headers. Duplicates are flagged before commit.
  4. Track daily targets — set a per-month daily target in the Calendar tab. Days that hit get a gold star + green border. Past months lock automatically.
  5. Backup — use the Backup button in the header anytime to download a JSON snapshot. Restore reloads from a backup.

Metric Glossary

Win Rate
Closed trades with Net P&L > 0 ÷ total decided trades. Break-evens (P&L = 0) are excluded from the denominator.
ROI %
(Net P&L ÷ Risk capital) × 100, per trade. Risk depends on strategy — see Strategy Glossary above.
Avg / Day % (Calendar footer)
Mean of each active day's capital-weighted return. For each day: Σ Net P&L ÷ Σ Risk × 100. Then averaged equally across active days.
Month % (Calendar footer)
Σ Net P&L for the month ÷ Σ Risk for the month × 100. The aggregate return on capital deployed.
Max Drawdown
Largest peak-to-trough decline in cumulative Net P&L. Shown in $ and as a % of the prior peak.
Profit Factor
Σ winning P&L ÷ Σ losing P&L (absolute). 1.5 is OK · 2.0 is good · 3.0+ is excellent. Below 1.0 means you're losing money.
Sortino Ratio
(Mean return − target) ÷ downside deviation. Like Sharpe but only penalizes downside volatility. Above 2 is strong.
Kelly Criterion
Optimal fraction of capital to risk per trade: f* = p − (1−p) ÷ R, where p = win rate, R = avg win ÷ avg loss. Most traders use ¼ or ½ Kelly to reduce volatility.
Theta Capture
For premium-collection strategies (CSP, CC): how much of the entry premium you actually kept after closing. Higher = better at letting time decay work for you.

Calendar & Targets

The Calendar tab visualizes each day's closed P&L on a month grid. Each day shows: trade count, gross win amount, gross loss amount, net P&L, and the day's capital-weighted Avg %. Hover near the trade count to see a tooltip listing every trade closed that day, sorted best to worst.

Daily Target
  • Set a daily target $ at the top of the Calendar tab. Days that hit it get a ⭐ and a green border.
  • Each month has its own target — change it for the current month, future months, but past months lock automatically and can't be edited.
  • Plan = trading days in the month (Mon-Fri) × that month's daily target. The Monthly Planned vs Actual table below the calendar tracks progress.

Tax Center

The Tax Center summarizes your closed trades by tax year, separating Section 1256 contracts (broad-based index options like SPX/NDX/RUT, plus futures) from regular trades.

  • Section 1256: 60% long-term / 40% short-term, marked-to-market at year-end, wash-sale rules don't apply.
  • Regular options & shares: Short-term capital gains by default unless held over 1 year. Standard wash-sale rules apply.
  • Tax reserve estimate: a configurable % of gross P&L set aside for quarterly estimates. Adjust the slider to your effective rate.

This is a tracking aid, not tax advice. Consult a CPA for filing.

Data, Privacy & Backup

  • Storage: Everything lives in your browser's localStorage on this device. No server. No account.
  • Hide $ button: Click 👁 Hide $ in the header to blur all dollar figures (useful for screen sharing). Click again to restore.
  • Backup: Click Backup to download a JSON file containing every trade and your settings. Save it somewhere safe.
  • Restore: Click Restore, pick a JSON backup. This replaces your current data — back up first if unsure.
  • Switching browsers / devices: use Backup → Restore to migrate. Data does not sync automatically.
  • Theme: ☀ / ☾ toggle in the header. The choice is remembered.
